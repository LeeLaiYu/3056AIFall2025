    Description

    About RSS

    Really Simple Syndication (“RSS”) is a file in eXtensible Markup Language (“XML”) format to deliver regular updates to users who are interested in specific content by subscribing the feed. Subscribers can view the subscribed feeds with any RSS reader or browser with built-in RSS reader.
